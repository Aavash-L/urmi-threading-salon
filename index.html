Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our threading services

Eyebrow threading is an ancient hair removal technique that originated in the Middle East and South Asia. It uses a twisted cotton thread to precisely remove hair from the follicle level, creating clean, defined brows. Unlike waxing, threading doesn't pull on the skin, making it ideal for sensitive skin types.

Threading typically lasts 3-5 weeks, depending on your hair growth cycle. Regular clients often find their hair grows back finer and sparser over time. We recommend coming every 2-3 weeks to maintain your perfect brow shape.

Threading is generally less painful than waxing because it doesn't adhere to the skin. Most clients describe it as a quick, mild discomfort similar to plucking but faster. Our technicians work efficiently to minimize any discomfort, and the sensation lessens with regular visits.

For best results, arrive with clean skin free of makeup and moisturizers. Avoid sun exposure and retinoid products 24 hours before your appointment. If it's your first time, we recommend letting your brows grow for at least 2 weeks so our technicians can see your natural shape.

We kindly request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. We understand emergencies happen, so please call us as soon as possible if you need to adjust your appointment.
